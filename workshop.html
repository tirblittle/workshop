Evolute Workshop

Freeform facades, from design to production

An overview of the complete geometry consultant workflow
March 15th - 17th, 2017 - Vienna

Description

Description

This workshop will allow participants to gain insights into the workflow of a typical freeform facade design project at Evolute. We will convert a freeform facade from an architectâ€™s design to the production data and drawing output needed by manufacturers for production of panels. The workshop will touch upon the following steps:

Optimization and refinement of the panelisation pattern

Creation of an optimized facade panelisation pattern based on architectural input, using techniques such as mesh parametrization, subdivision mesh modelling and optimization.

Manufacturing techniques

Manufacturing techniques used for curved facade panels (glass, metal, GRC, GRP) and their geometric limitations.

Curved panel simplification

Simplification of double curved panels by replacing them with flat, cold-bent, or cylindrical panels, within acceptable tolerance.

Clustering

Reduction of panel production cost by clustering similar panels within acceptable tolerance.

Production drawings and data

Automated output of drawings and production data for flat and cylindrical IGUs, beams, metal profiles, and other facade elements.

The participants will have the chance to solve problems using their own experience and skills. Evolute will also provide an introduction to its software suite and show how to use it in the context of a real-life project. A one-year licence for EvoluteTools PRO, D.LOFT, T.MAP will be offered complimentary to all the participants.

Team

Team

Evolute GmbH is a high-tech company founded as a spin-off from Vienna University of Technology in 2008. Since its foundation, Evolute has gained a wealth of practical expertise in enabling building projects featuring complex geometry and in solving geometric problems in automation and production technology. Evolute has built up a dedicated and experienced team of mathematicians, computer scientists, and engineers. We offer creativity and are at the same time grounded in practical competence. Our references include projects from highly technical to highly spectacular.
